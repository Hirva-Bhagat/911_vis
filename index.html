The efficient management and provision of 911 calls (an emergency telephone number in the United States) have been consistently challenging. As a result, several stakeholder groups have been trying to collect, use, and analyze the relevant information in the last years to resolve the issues such as inadequate resources, lag time for a proper response, and other consequences link. Achieving a systemized plan for a more effective management plan will benefit all stakeholders providing service and the residents and visitors. Such a proper plan certainly requires a comprehensive understanding of the statistics showing the information, which motivates a compelling information visualization, which our group has been trying to provide.
